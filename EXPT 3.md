
## CHARACTERISTICS OF RTD
# AIM
To study the characteristics of Resistance temperature detector (RTD)

# APPARATUS REQUIRED 

1. RTD
2. Hot bath 1 Nos
3. Multimeter
   
# EXPERIMENTAL SETUP

# THEORY
An RTD senses temperature by the change of electrical resistance of a metal element with temperature
(positive temperature coefficient).RTDs are constructed using metals with a positive temperature
coefficient of resistance, meaning resistance increases with temperature.

# PROCEDURE
1. Fill the hot bath with water until the level the thermometer bulb and sensor end is completely
immersed.
2. Connect the terminal ends to the multimeter terminals.
3. Heat the water till it boils at 100 deg C.
4. Switch off the water bath and allow the system to cool.
5. During the process of colling tabulate resistance values corresponding to the respective
temperature.
6. Plot the graph and determine the sensitivity.
7. Tabulate readings and plot graph to study characteristics;
i)Resistance-Temperature for RTD
